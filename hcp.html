Glozzle Hand Cleaning Paste

GLOZZLE Hand cleaning paste delivers a thorough cleanse with its powerful yet gentle formula, designed to tackle stubborn dirt, grease, and grime. The exfoliating agents effectively scrub away impurities while softening and smoothing the skin. Enriched with moisturizing ingredients, it prevents dryness. How To Use

Thoroughly rub the paste into dry hands until the dirt has been released from the skin. Next, add a little water and scrub thoroughly. Rinse the hands with plenty of water and dry them as usual. Reapply if necessary.
